Taxi boat Prne

Boat excursions – sunset(dolphins), swimming tours, panorama


We are a small cruising yacht boat that fits up to 12 persons, and we are doing boat excursions around Brijuni national park islands. On our boat You can sit on the front of the boat while cruising or stay under the shade in the back, and our boat is also equipped with toilette and cabin, which makes it perfect for sea cruising. The boat is equipped with toilette. Our team (captain and tourist guide) operate for more then 10 years, if you need anything else you can speak with the tourist guide on the boat. Services & Pricing

Choose an option that suits you!

We operate every day, contact us for more info!

Sunset(Dolphins)

Cruise through aquatorium of Brijuni national park, during the sunset. Excursion is 90-150 minutes long, depending on how lucky we are. On normal occasions we see them more then 75% of the time.

150 HRK (20 EUR)

per person

*Children are half price

Panorama

Cruise through aquatorium of Brijuni national park. Cruise is 90 minutes long. You’ll see all 14 beautiful islands from the boat, and at the end of the tour, you can have a swimming break at one island (Min. 2 hours).

150 HRK (20 EUR)

per person

*Children are half price

Private tours

You decide! On private tours you can choose if you want to spend your time swimming(from the boat or on the island St. Jeorlim), or cruising. On private tours you have boat for yourself or just your family or friends!

from 1500 HRK (200 EUR)

per tour

*Prices may vary for some special requests.
